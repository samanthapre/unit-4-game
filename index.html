You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four different balls below.

  • By clicking on a balls, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  • You win the game by matching your total score to the random number.
  • You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
  • The value of each ball is hidden from you until you click on it.
  • Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each ball.
